Information Technology

Title

Information Technology (IT) refers to the use of computers, software, networks, and other digital technologies to store, process, transmit, and manage data and information. IT encompasses a wide range of activities, including computer hardware and software development, network administration, database management, cybersecurity, and IT support services. It plays a crucial role in modern society by enabling communication, enhancing productivity, and facilitating access to information across various industries and sectors.
